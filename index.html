AI Protection Layer

You Become Oversight,
Not Technician.

Our AI Protection Layer handles detection, triage, and remediation automatically — so your team spends zero time on routine IT.

  • 01

    Alert Normalization

    AI consolidates signals from all monitoring sources, eliminating noise and surfacing only real threats.

  • 02

    Intelligent Ticket Triage

    Incoming issues are automatically classified by severity, type, and urgency — routed to the right resolution path instantly.

  • 03

    Auto-Remediation Logic

    Known issues are resolved automatically before they impact users — patches applied, services restarted, threats quarantined.

  • 04

    Auto-Generated Guardian Reports

    Monthly executive summaries generated automatically — showing what was protected, resolved, and optimized.

  • 05

    Knowledge Base Automation

    Every resolved ticket enriches the knowledge base, making future resolutions faster and the system smarter over time.

  • 06

    Voice-Triggered Support

    Users can initiate support requests via voice command — the AI logs, classifies, and routes the request automatically.
